L1 Staking

Blazt Chain only became possible this year following Binance . BSC yield from L1 staking, initially Lido, is automatically transferred to users via rebasing BSC on the L2.

T-BILL yield

Users who bridge stablecoins receive USDB, Blast's auto-rebasing stablecoin. The yield for USDB comes from MakerDAO's on-chain T-Bill protocol. USDB can be redeemed for USDC when bridging back to BSC.

Why a new L2 ?

L2s today do not have this yield. Incorporating BSC and stablecoin yield natively requires a new L2 designed from the ground up. Blast is an EVM-compatible, optimistic rollup that raises the baseline yield for users and developers without changing the experience cryptonatives expect.

Blazt is an L2 with native yield for BSC and stablecoins. Yield makes it possible to create new revenue streams and provide novel rewards for end-users.
